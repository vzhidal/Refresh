LESS ENERGY, MORE SAVINGS

The Refresh dispenser is one lean machine, eliminating fuel used to truck* water. Also, the electricity required to run the machine is far less. All this adds up to significant cost savings throughout the bottled beverage supply chain.

*Did you know that 1/3 of the energy used to produce your bottled beverage comes from transportation alone?

OUR STORY

Our goal at Refresh is to make sustainability easy for everyone. We are a team of environmental pragmatists out to change the world. We're humble, but irrationally ambitious and always optimistic.
