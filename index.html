ABOUT US

Our goal at Refresh is to make sustainability easy for everyone. We are a team of environmental pragmatists out to change the world. We're humble, but irrationally ambitious and always optimistic.

  • SEAN GRUNDY is the co-founder and CEO of Refresh. He worked as an environmental conservationist in Southwest China, and more recently worked in energy efficiency at a plastics manufacturing/recycling company. He is a big proponent of furry animals and Brazilian jiu-jitsu. Sean holds an MBA from MIT Sloan, and obtained his BA from Princeton University.

  • ELIZA BECTON is the co-founder and head of design at Refresh. She is a life-long sailor, a puzzle nerd and feels passionately that we need to make products that tackle ambitious environmental problems. She holds a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Yale University and a Master of Industrial Design from the Rhode Island School of Design.

  • FRANK LEE is the co-founder and head of engineering at Refresh. His background is in consumer products, having worked in manufacturing and brand management for Procter & Gamble. He is a purple belt in Brazilian jiu jitsu and enjoys Berimbolos. Frank is an MIT Sloan MBA, and graduated with a BS in Chemical Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin.
